The Thames River is the second largest river in the UK and the longest river in the England [1].

In "Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix" [2], Harry and the members of the Order of the Phoenix are transferred together from his aunt's house to Sirius' house. During the transfer, they rode flying broomsticks, flew over the Thames River, and overlooked many of London's landmarks.

The Thames River is located in the southwest of England. It is steeped in history and known as the "Mother River" of England. Many of London's most famous sights are located on both sides of the Thames, such as Big Ben and the London Eye.
