Borough Market is a food market with a lively atmosphere and has been around for over two hundred years. The market has many countries' delicious food, such as paella and Thai coconut cake. Borough Market is also near London Bridge and the Shard Building.

Address: 8 Southwark St, London SE1 1TL, UK
